Providing MyBooking

  • Easy to use Covid-19 Response to desk booking platform to maintain social distancing at work.
  • Fully Customisable and Controllable to meet operational needs and organise the flow of staff across the network.
  • Staff simply select office location, required floor, bank of desks and specific desk and book according to their required date and time.
  • Full Reporting functions to allow compliance with Government Track and Trace Initiative so Operations know exactly who is where at any moment.
  • Staff reported greater feeling of security and safety knowing they could allocate their desk when they require being in the office.
